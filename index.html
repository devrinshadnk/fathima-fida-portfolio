02 — About

Designing with purpose & passion

I believe the best enterprise products feel simple — even when the systems behind them are anything but.

I'm Fathima Fida M, a UI/UX Designer currently at UST, where I design enterprise web applications, data-driven dashboards, and Agentic AI–integrated platforms. Previously at Aarrrmor Digital Agency, I led website redesigns, built design systems, and created marketing landing pages that improved brand recognition and conversions.

My edge? I use AI-powered design tools — Figma Make, Claude, and Canva AI — to accelerate ideation, rapid prototyping, and design iterations. But every design decision is rooted in real user research and usability testing, not just speed.

Design Philosophy

"Complexity is the designer's problem, not the user's. My role is to absorb the complexity so users never have to."
