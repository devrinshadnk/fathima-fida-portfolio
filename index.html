02 — About

Designing with purpose & passion

I believe the best enterprise products feel simple — even when the systems behind them are anything but.

I'm Fathima Fida M, a UI/UX Designer currently at UST, where I design AI-powered enterprise platforms, data-driven dashboards, and complex workflow systems. Previously at Aarrrmor Digital Agency, I led website redesigns and built design systems that improved brand recognition and conversion rates.

My edge? I sit at the intersection of Agentic AI and human-centered design. I use AI-powered tools to accelerate ideation — but every design decision is rooted in real user research, not just speed.

Design Philosophy
